# PRODUCT.md

## Product

"去K书" — a study room booking mini-program/app for college students and young professionals to find, browse, and reserve seats in local study spaces.

## Users

- Primary: College students aged 18-25 in Chinese tier-2/3 cities
- Secondary: Young professionals preparing for exams or certifications
- Context: Mobile-first, used in transit and on-campus, quick session flows

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

Discovery and booking of study spaces: browse rooms by location, view real-time seat availability, select time slots, and complete reservations in under 30 seconds.

## Brand Tone

Clean, efficient, trustworthy. Not playful. The interface should feel like a well-organized tool that respects the user's time.

## Anti-References

- Heavy gamification or loyalty-point clutter
- Social feed features (comments, likes, shares on room pages)
- Dark mode (not requested, not the audience expectation)
- Overly decorative illustrations or mascot characters

## Strategic Principles

1. Speed to seat: minimize taps between "open app" and "seat reserved"
2. Information density over decoration: show availability, hours, and pricing prominently
3. Familiar mobile patterns: users are fluent in WeChat mini-programs and iOS apps
4. Trust through transparency: real photos, real ratings, real availability counts
